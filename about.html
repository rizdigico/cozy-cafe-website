Our Story

About Cozy Cafe

Where Comfort Meets Community

Cozy Cafe started with a simple idea: create a space where you can slow down, enjoy great coffee, and feel at home. Nestled in Cadillac, Michigan, we've been serving our community with freshly brewed coffee, homemade pastries, and hearty meals made with love.

Whether you're a student cramming for exams, a professional looking for a quiet workspace, or a family enjoying a weekend treat — there's a seat waiting for you at Cozy Cafe.
